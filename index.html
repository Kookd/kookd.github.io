About Research Projects

David Kook

I am a 1st year PhD student in Quantitative Biomedical Sciences at Dartmouth College, currently rotating with Dr. Daniel Schultz. I am interested in using technology to improve diagnostic accuracy and tailor treatments. My work involves designing or implementing statistical and machine learning frameworks to tackle problems in precision medicine such as, integrating multi-omics to understand the effects of environemntal toxins like arsenic on health, automating radiographic image segmentations for head and neck cancer, and identifying sub-groups to determine dose-dependent reponses.
