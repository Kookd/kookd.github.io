About Research Projects

David Kook

I am a biomedical data scientist interested in using technology to improve diagnostic accuracy and individualize treatments. My work involves designing or implementing statistical and machine learning frameworks to tackle problems in precision medicine such as, integrating multi-omics to understand the effects of environemntal toxins like arsenic on health, automating radiographic image segmentations for head and neck cancer, and identifying sub-groups to determine dose-dependent immunological reponses.
